Reset

About the season reset

The season reset is a crucial moment in Last War. Some progress is kept, other parts restart, and proper preparation prevents losing advantages built over months of gameplay.

Checklist before reset:

  • Spend resources that will be lost before the reset date
  • Complete pending missions and events to maximize rewards
  • Check with the alliance which heroes and gear will be kept
  • Plan your progression strategy for the new season
